About Hamz

Hamz Swim School is a premier swimming institution committed to developing confident, skilled swimmers through a holistic and accessible approach. Founded and led by Coach Magoba Brian, widely recognized as Coach Hamz, the academy draws its name from his passion and dedication to the sport.

We offer a dynamic blend of online and in-person swim instruction, making it possible for learners from various locations and backgrounds to benefit from world-class coaching. Our curriculum focuses heavily on technical excellence, with structured programs in stroke development, swim mechanics, and endurance training—tailored for both beginners and advanced swimmers.

As part of our mission to build a strong swimming culture, Hamz Swim School is also a trusted supplier and shipper of professional swim equipment, ensuring our athletes and partners have access to the tools they need to thrive.

Beyond training, we are proud organizers of competitive swimming galas, nurturing sportsmanship, talent discovery, and community engagement through well-coordinated aquatic events.

At Hamz Swim School, we don’t just teach swimming—we build swimmers, nurture discipline, and champion a lifelong relationship with the water. My mission is to ensure water safety and excellence across the community.
